Merlot

Full bodied, tastes like Cherry, Plum, Chocolate, Bay Leaf & Vanilla

Merlot is produced in France from a White Grape in Bordeux and also Tuscany Italy.

Pairs well with Pizza, tomato based pastas and creams, also makes amazing sauces and gravy.

Makes for a great beginner wine
